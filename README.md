# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical business problems in the telecom industry. This project aims to predict whether a customer is likely to leave the company using Machine Learning and provide business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies lose significant revenue when customers discontinue their services. The objective of this project is to identify customers who are likely to churn so that retention strategies can be implemented proactively.

## 📊 Evaluation Metrics

- Accuracy
- Precision
- Recall
- F1-Score

The final model was selected based on Recall because correctly identifying customers who are likely to churn is more important than simply maximizing overall accuracy.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Month-to-Month contract customers have a higher churn rate.
- Monthly Charges significantly influence churn.
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave.
- Contract type is one of the strongest indicators of customer churn.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

- Offer discounts or loyalty plans for Month-to-Month customers.
- Provide personalized retention offers for high-risk customers.
- Introduce special retention programs for senior citizens (if supported by the analysis).
- Use the churn prediction model to identify at-risk customers and launch proactive retention campaigns.
